About Bess

Is your home in need of a fresh new life? Are you visualizing a remodeling job so you can sell at top price? Do you have an office or a room you want to bring joy to? How good would it be to have someone to not only plan with you, but shop alongside you for the right colors, textures and furnishings?

Bess Tracy is a Colorado native, with a heart to change atmospheres and bring fun, harmony, peace, function, and beauty to your life. She is also a real estate broker with Team Vivi, specializing in the greater Denver area. Her experience in home and commercial construction, real estate and interior design is a culmination of her life-long love of creating beautiful spaces. Growing up in a family of log home builders, Bess learned early about work ethic while developing a keen eye toward process, trends, and an ability to work with clients and subcontractors. A graduate of The Interior Designer’s Institute, Bess was chosen to appear as both real estate broker and interior designer of a Denver home on HGTV’s House Hunter’s Renovation. Bess can help you decorate by rearranging your own furnishings, or she can help you create a new design with construction, finding the right pieces, and use of color and texture. Bess will work with you to support your vision for any space. You’ll get custom design to suit who you are.

My Process

  • Meet & Agree
  • Idea & Concept
  • Design & Create
  • Build & Install
Bess will meet you for an initial overview, followed by a drawn schematic and estimate of costs. Once an agreement is signed, any contractors are scheduled; shopping days are put on the calendar; and the work can begin. First-Time Consult: $300
Bess will give you choices of style and trends, then help you develop your own unique plan that fits your budget. This is where the fun begins. You will never forget your shopping trip with Bess. She’s knowledgable, focused, happy, and you will go home feeling excited about what’s to come.
Once the concept, furnishings, and budget are decided, your home will begin to evolve into a finished work of art that fills your soul where you can be proud to host guests, or just cozy up with a cup of coffee and feel creative, loved, and truly at home.
For those projects requiring installations of new appliances, removal or addition of walls, and any other new construction, Bess will work with you on the best times to schedule work, and will make every effort to make the design process an enjoyable experience.
